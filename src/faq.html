How secure is my data?

What happens if I forget my password?

‚ûï

Why do I need to enter my password every time?

‚ûï

‚ö†Ô∏è This is by design and cannot be changed

Holy Private Bookmarks is built on a zero-trust security model. Requiring password entry every time is not a limitation‚Äîit's the core security feature.

Why this is non-negotiable:

  • True zero-knowledge: We never store your password, session tokens, or decryption keys
  • Defense in depth: Even with physical access to your computer, your bookmarks remain protected
  • No single point of failure: There are no backdoors, recovery keys, or password reset options
  • Browser security limitations: Chrome extensions cannot securely persist authentication between sessions

Security benefits:

  • Protection against theft: If your device is stolen, your bookmarks remain encrypted
  • No forensic traces: Nothing is stored in browser storage that could be recovered
  • Malware resistance: Even keyloggers only capture one-time password entry

üí° Pro tip: Use a password manager like Bitwarden or KeePass. With auto-fill, entering your password takes just 2 seconds while maintaining maximum security.

Remember: Every time you enter your password, you're verifying that YOU are the only person who can access your private bookmarks. This is the price of true privacy.

How do I add bookmarks?

‚ûï

You can add bookmarks in several ways:

What's the difference between popup and manager?

‚ûï

Popup: Quick access for adding/viewing bookmarks. Automatically locks after 10 minutes.

Manager: Full-featured interface with search, grid view, advanced editing, and folder management. Also auto-locks for security.
